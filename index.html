Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

1978
Foundation of EAR Bank
Established in Manila with ₱50M capitalization and a vision to democratize banking for Filipinos across all economic strata.
1985
Nationwide Expansion
Opened 40 new branches across Visayas and Mindanao, reaching underserved communities for the first time.
1998
BSP Universal Banking License
Granted full universal banking status, enabling EAR to offer investment banking, trust services, and foreign currency operations.
2008
Navigated the Global Financial Crisis
Maintained zero depositor loss through the 2008 crisis, reinforcing our reputation for stability and conservative risk management.
2018
Digital Transformation Launch
Launched EAR Mobile — the most downloaded banking app in the Philippines with over 1M downloads in its first year.
2024
₱480B in Total Assets
Achieved record total assets exceeding ₱480 billion, serving over 3.2 million active clients nationwide.

Our Foundation

Vision, Mission, Goals & Core Values

The principles and aspirations that guide every decision we make at EAR Bank.

Who We Are

Vision & Mission

Our vision charts where we are headed; our mission defines how we get there — together with every client we serve.

Vision
To be the most trusted bank in the Philippines.

EAR Bank envisions a future where every Filipino — whether in the heart of Metro Manila or the farthest barangay — has access to world-class financial services built on integrity, innovation, and genuine care. We strive to be the financial institution that Filipinos turn to first, trust completely, and recommend proudly.

Mission
Empowering lives through responsible and inclusive banking.

We are committed to delivering innovative, accessible, and responsible financial solutions that empower individuals, families, and businesses to achieve their dreams. EAR Bank creates lasting value by building meaningful relationships, upholding the highest standards of integrity, and continuously improving the lives of the communities we serve.

Strategic Direction

Goals & Objectives

Six strategic pillars that translate our vision into measurable outcomes for our clients, partners, and the nation.

1
Financial Inclusion
Expand access to basic banking, credit, and savings products to underserved communities across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao through digital channels and micro-branch expansion.
2
Customer-First Excellence
Achieve a Net Promoter Score (NPS) of 80+ by 2027 through personalized service, faster transaction processing, and a seamless omnichannel banking experience.
3
Digital Transformation
Migrate 70% of all transactions to digital platforms by 2026, launching AI-assisted personal finance tools, real-time fraud detection, and open banking APIs.
4
Sustainable Growth
Grow total assets to ₱500 billion by 2028 while maintaining a capital adequacy ratio above BSP requirements and expanding our loan portfolio responsibly.
5
Corporate Social Responsibility
Invest ₱2 billion in community development, financial literacy programs, and green financing initiatives supporting the Philippines' sustainable development goals.
6
Talent & Culture
Develop a future-ready workforce by upskilling 100% of employees in digital competencies, fostering a culture of diversity, equity, and continuous learning by 2026.
